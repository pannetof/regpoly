    Cellular Automata based Resource Efficient Maximally Equidistributed Pseudo-Random Number Generators¶

    Bhuvaneswari A and Kamalika Bhattacharjee, 2026. Cellular Automata based Resource Efficient Maximally Equidistributed Pseudo-Random Number Generators. arXiv preprint.

    DOI

    BibTeX key: BhuvaneswariBhattacharjee2026CA

    Tags: cellular-automata F2-linear ME combined time-spacing

    Abstract¶

    Light-weight combined PRNGs built from 2 linear maximal-length elementary cellular automata (rules 90 and 150 only, null boundary) with time-spacing s ∈ {2..10}. Component CA sizes 31 ≤ k ≤ 128. The paper shows that classical CA-PRNGs from the literature fail maximal equidistribution, then constructs ~370 combined CA-PRNGs that attain ME with periods close to 2^(k1+k2), comparable to the Mersenne Twister in speed and surpassing it in ME / Dieharder / SmallCrush / BigCrush performance.
